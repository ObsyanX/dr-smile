## 🖱️ Part 5: Click Signal Strategy (Advanced)

This tells Google that people are finding and engaging with your site from search results — a strong ranking signal.

### Daily Routine (5 minutes/day)
1. Open Google (Private/Incognito window)
2. Search: "best dental clinic in Madhyamgram"
3. Find your website in results → CLICK IT
4. Browse at least 2–3 pages (stay 2+ minutes)
5. Scroll slowly — don't leave immediately
6. Repeat for: "dentist dum dum", "root canal madhyamgram", "dentist near me"

### Ask Staff/Friends to Do This Too
Get 5–10 people to do this daily for the first 4 weeks. This sends strong "click-through rate" signals to Google.
